About Us

Our Journey

Welcome to the Video Game Graphics Evolution project! We’re a team of enthusiasts from SUNY Oswego—Mariah, Ross, and Dominic—passionate about the dynamic world of video game graphics. This project explores the visual journey from the early days of pixel art to today’s stunning, hyper-realistic graphics.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and immersive experience showcasing the evolution of video game graphics. Through timelines, galleries, and insightful content, we aim to bring to light the artistry and innovation that have shaped the gaming visuals we enjoy today.

Meet the Team

Mariah

Mariah combines her passion for gaming and history to create engaging timelines and articles, bringing context to each era of video game graphics and explaining its significance to modern gamers.

Ross

With a background in computer science, Ross dives into the technical evolution of graphics in gaming. He curates content on key graphics innovations that transformed gaming visuals over the decades.

Dominic

Dominic is a design enthusiast with a keen eye for detail. he leads the visual design aspects of the project, ensuring the aesthetics resonate with the history and evolution of video game art.

Contact Us

Have questions or ideas? Reach out through our Contact Page! We’d love to hear from fellow enthusiasts and gamers interested in the evolution of game graphics.
